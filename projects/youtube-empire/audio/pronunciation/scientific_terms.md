## Script Format

Use this format in scripts:
```
Plants make their own food through photosynthesis (foe-toh-SIN-theh-sis). 
That's a big word that means "putting together with light!"
```

For complex terms:
```
The mitochondria (my-toh-KON-dree-uh) are like tiny power plants inside your cells.
```
