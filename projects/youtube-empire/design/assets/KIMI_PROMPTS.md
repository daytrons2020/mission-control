## GENERAL TIPS FOR AI IMAGE GENERATION

### For Best Results:

1. **Be Specific:** Include exact colors, mood, and style
2. **Add Dimensions:** Specify 1280x720 for thumbnails, 2560x1440 for channel art
3. **Include Quality Tags:** "4K", "8K", "high quality", "detailed"
4. **Specify Style:** "anime", "watercolor", "cartoon", "photorealistic"
5. **Add Purpose:** "YouTube thumbnail", "channel art"

### Style Keywords That Work Well:

- **Lo-fi:** "aesthetic", "muted colors", "film grain", "nostalgic"
- **Kids Content:** "cartoon", "bright colors", "friendly", "simple shapes"
- **Sleep/Ambient:** "peaceful", "dark", "serene", "cinematic"
- **Stories:** "whimsical", "storybook", "watercolor", "magical"

### To Avoid:

- Overly complex scenes
- Too many subjects
- Conflicting lighting directions
- Text in images (AI struggles with text)
