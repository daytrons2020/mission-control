## 💬 Communication Tips

**Introduce Yourself:**
> "Hi, I'm [Name], a respiratory therapy student. I'm here to help with your breathing treatment today."

**SBAR for Report:**
- **S**ituation — What's happening right now?
- **B**ackground — What's the context?
- **A**ssessment — What do you think is going on?
- **R**ecommendation — What do you suggest?

**Teach-Back Method:**
> "Can you tell me in your own words how to use your inhaler?"

---

## 📱 Social Media Rules

❌ **NEVER:**
- Post about specific patients
- Share photos from patient care areas
- Discuss cases in public (elevators, cafeteria)

✅ **ALWAYS:**
- Keep work at work
- When in doubt, don't post
- Remember: HIPAA violations end careers

---

## 📝 Documentation Reminder

> **"If it wasn't charted, it didn't happen"**

- Document in real-time when possible
- Be objective and factual
- Never chart for someone else
- Ask for help with the EMR
