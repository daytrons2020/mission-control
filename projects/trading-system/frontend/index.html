🎯 Pattern Recognition Cards
💀 RUG PULL bearish
▼ FLUSH
Setup
Positive GEX ceiling directly above a negative (purple) node. Price near or above the ceiling.
Entry
Short when price taps the yellow ceiling and fails to hold. Puts at the ceiling strike.
Exit
Cover at next major support node or king strike below.
Win Rate: ~70% when VIX rising + negative GEX regime
🚀 SLINGSHOT bullish
▲ LAUNCH
Setup
Negative GEX ceiling above positive GEX floor — the inverse of a Rug Pull. Price holds the yellow floor while VIX declines.
Entry
Long at the yellow floor node. Look for "Clear Skies" (zero upside resistance) = slingshot velocity.
Exit
Target King strike. Clear Skies = let runners ride. Scale out at next Gatekeeper.
Win Rate: ~70% with VIX falling + Clear Skies + no junk nodes
🛡️ GATEKEEPER neutral
🚫 REJECT
Setup
Large positive GEX node between spot and the king strike. Price approaching it.
Entry
Fade at the gatekeeper level. Sell calls or buy puts on 1st/2nd touch.
Exit
Take profit on bounce back toward origin. Flip bias on 3rd test.
Win Rate: ~65% on 1st test, ~45% on 2nd, flip on 3rd
⚡ WHIPSAW neutral
↕ PING-PONG
Setup
+GEX ceiling and -GEX floor within 1.5% of each other. Equal-ish magnitude.
Entry
Limit orders at both nodes. Long at floor, short at ceiling.
Exit
Opposite node. Stop if either node breaks on 3rd+ test.
Win Rate: ~60% per bounce. 3:1 R/R possible (SPX case study)
🎯 FLOOR BOUNCE bullish
⬆ BOUNCE
Setup
SPY/QQQ hit a major -GEX floor node with zero volume/nodes beneath it.
Entry
Long at the floor node. Use "Reset Entry" if initial tap missed.
Exit
Trim 30% on first pop, 50% more after pullback holds. Trail final 20%.
Win Rate: ~70% with "Empty Basement" + structural confluence
🏀 BEACH BALL bullish
⬆ SPRING UP
Setup
Strong negative GEX regime. Spot pushed well below king strike into a -GEX pit.
Entry
Lowball bid at or near the deepest -GEX node. Use the ±5 pt rule for SPX.
Exit
Target the king strike as the "magnet" for the reversal. Scale out in thirds.
Win Rate: ~80%+ highest reward potential (3,000%+ on the Feb 5 case study)
🌈 RAINBOW ROAD neutral
⚠️ AVOID
Setup
VEX magnitude exceeds GEX by 1.5x. Typically triggered by VIX spike or news event.
Entry
Trade in the direction of the dominant VEX node. These are distant magnets.
Exit
At the VEX target node. Watch for node coming back to you on spikes.
Win Rate: ~60% — High-velocity moves. VEX overrides GEX during air pockets.
📈 TREND directional
→ DRIFT
Setup
King strike >1% away from current spot price. Clear directional bias.
Entry
Trade in the direction of the king strike. Calls if king is above, puts if below.
Exit
Scale out as price approaches king. Watch for gatekeeper forming along the way.
Win Rate: ~70% on days with clear macro alignment (no "Window of Weakness")
